Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Babu & Booshik LLC ("we", "our", "us") operates the JARTD mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

JARTD is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Player Names: Names entered during gameplay are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Subscription Information: If you subscribe to JARTD Premium, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect or store your payment information. We receive confirmation of your subscription status through RevenueCat to manage access to premium content.
  • Promo Keys: If you redeem a promotional key, the key value is stored locally on your device and may be validated against our server to confirm eligibility.
  • Game Settings: Your preferences (intensity level, selected packs, etc.) are stored locally on your device.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information (email, phone number, address).
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels.
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service to manage subscriptions:

  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status and entitlements. RevenueCat may collect anonymous identifiers to track subscription status. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

All game data (player names, settings, game history) is stored locally on your device using standard device storage. This data is not backed up to our servers. Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.

5. Subscriptions & In-App Purchases

JARTD offers premium subscription plans (monthly and annual) to unlock additional content packs. All subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store or Google Play. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. We do not collect or store your payment information. Please refer to the respective platform privacy policies:

  • Apple: apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Google: policies.google.com/privacy

6. Children's Privacy

JARTD is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 17. The App contains references to alcohol consumption and is rated 17+ (iOS) / Mature (Android). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 17. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the App, please contact us and we will take steps to remove any data.

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not operate servers that collect user data, the security of your information depends on your device's security settings. We recommend using a passcode or biometric lock on your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no personal data for us to access, modify, or delete. All data stored locally on your device can be removed by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data through your device settings.
